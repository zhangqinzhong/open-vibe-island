Readout

Why This Version Works

Orbit gives the spark a little personality without touching the mascot body. It reads as “working” immediately, but it is also the busiest of the three motion families.

  • Body stays fixed on an 8x8 grid.
  • Spark stays fixed on a 4x4 grid.
  • The spark only shifts by one cell between frames.
  • No scaling, no blur, no rotation, no sub-pixel movement.
